# Assignment1
Assignment1_data.csv contains data from 5 popular books. It states the title, the author, the year of publication, and the genre of the books. 
The source of the data is Google Books (https://books.google.com). 
The structure of the csv file is showed in the table underneath. 

| Title | Author | Year | Genre |
|-------|--------|------|----------|
|The Title of the Book | The Author of the Book | The Year the Book was Published | The Genre Google Books Places These Books in |
